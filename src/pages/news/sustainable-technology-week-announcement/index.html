Introducing Sustainable Technology Week

A public initiative focused on digital sustainability, awareness, and practical climate-conscious technology action.

VANCOUVER, British Columbia — October 20, 2025

Oasis of Change today announced the launch of Sustainable Technology Week, a public initiative dedicated to raising awareness and driving practical action on digital sustainability, ethical technology, and climate-conscious innovation.

The week was established through an official proclamation by the City of Vancouver, recognizing October 20 to 27, 2025 as Sustainable Technology Week and underscoring the importance of responsible digital infrastructure in the transition to a lower-carbon economy.

Programming centres on education, public conversations, and partner events exploring the environmental cost of digital systems and the practical steps organizations can take to reduce it.

“Sustainable technology is not an abstract idea. It is a set of choices we can make today that compound over every page load, every device, and every decision.”

— Oasis of Change team

The initiative complements Oasis of Change’s ongoing work with nonprofits, community organizations, and municipal partners to lower the carbon footprint of websites, digital services, and end-of-life hardware.

Sustainable Technology Week is designed to recur annually, building a long-term platform for public education and partner collaboration around responsible technology.

Partner Perspective

“Recognizing Sustainable Technology Week sends a clear signal that digital sustainability belongs in every conversation about the future of our city.”

— City of Vancouver proclamation

The organization invites residents, businesses, and civic institutions to participate in the week’s programming and to carry its message into their own work year-round.

About Oasis of Change, Inc.

Oasis of Change, Inc. is a Vancouver-based nonprofit focused on digital sustainability, low-carbon web development, and measurable environmental impact. Through its work, the organization develops technology and digital infrastructure that support communities while advancing transparency, tree planting, and public engagement around sustainability.
