Welcoming Nigel and Catherine to Our Board of Directors

As Oasis of Change continues to grow, we’re excited to welcome Nigel and Catherine to our Board of Directors. Their support comes at an important time as we expand our work in digital sustainability, accessibility, and environmental impact.

Over the past few years, Oasis of Change has grown from an idea into a global initiative — helping organizations build more energy-efficient websites while supporting reforestation and environmental restoration efforts.

As this work expands, strong leadership becomes even more important. Nigel and Catherine bring valuable experience and perspective that will help guide our direction and ensure we continue building responsibly and effectively.

From the Oasis of Change team

“This is an exciting step forward for us. Having Nigel and Catherine involved strengthens our ability to grow while staying focused on meaningful, measurable impact.”

Together, we’re continuing to push forward with projects that make the digital world more efficient — reducing emissions, improving accessibility, and helping organizations operate more sustainably online.

We’re grateful to have Nigel and Catherine join us on this journey and look forward to what we’ll build together.
