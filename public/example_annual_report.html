Overview

During the 2025–2026 fiscal year, Oasis of Change continued to develop as a mission-driven organization focused on digital sustainability, public education, and practical impact. This period reflected a growing emphasis on building projects that connect environmental responsibility with technology, accessibility, and long-term systems change.

Organizational Progress

Over the course of the fiscal year, Oasis of Change strengthened its public-facing identity, expanded core website content, and continued shaping how its work is presented through projects, case studies, annual reporting, and mission-focused digital communication. This included further work on transparency-related pages, organizational storytelling, and how the nonprofit presents its impact to partners, supporters, and the broader public.

The year also reflected continued development across initiatives and subsidiaries that connect environmental thinking with digital practice. These efforts helped clarify the organization’s structure while creating stronger pathways for future growth.

Key Initiatives

Several projects and initiatives were active or in development during the 2025–2026 fiscal year. These included sustainable web development work, public education efforts related to digital sustainability, transparency and impact reporting, and the advancement of mission-aligned projects designed to support long-term public benefit.

The year also included progress on project pages and supporting infrastructure for initiatives such as Sustainable Technology Week, the Web-Ready Access Platform, Web-Ready, and the Vancouver Centre for AI Safety, Sustainability and Ethics.

Impact Highlights

Oasis of Change continued to communicate and build on its environmental and digital impact through tree planting transparency, sustainable website case studies, and public-facing sustainability statements. During this period, the organization continued to highlight measurable outcomes such as large-scale tree planting support and major reductions in emissions, energy use, and water use on select website projects.

These impact areas remain central to the organization’s work because they help connect abstract ideas about sustainability to visible, measurable results.

Looking Ahead

Looking ahead, Oasis of Change plans to continue strengthening its website, annual reporting, transparency practices, project ecosystem, and public education efforts. Future years are expected to build on the foundations established during 2025–2026, with continued attention to sustainable technology, inclusive digital design, accountability, and mission-aligned growth.
