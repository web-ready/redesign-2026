Web-Ready Access Platform

The Web-Ready Access Platform helps nonprofits strengthen their digital foundations through digital sustainability, web accessibility, and access to in-kind technology grants.

Platform Focus

Built to help nonprofits improve how they build, manage, and grow online.

Tracks

3

Digital sustainability, web accessibility, and in-kind technology grants.

Audience

Nonprofits

Designed to support organizations that need stronger digital capacity and clearer guidance.

Approach

Integrated

Combines sustainability, accessibility, and technology enablement instead of treating them separately.

Goal

Access

Make strong digital practices and key tools more reachable for mission-driven organizations.

Why it matters

Many nonprofits are expected to meet modern digital standards without the time, funding, or technical support needed to do it well. The Web-Ready Access Platform was built to help close that gap.

Core Tracks

Three connected areas of support

01

Digital sustainability

Helps organizations understand how websites and digital systems can be made lower-impact, more efficient, and more responsible in how they use energy and resources.

02

Web accessibility

Supports nonprofits in building digital experiences that are more inclusive and easier to use for people with disabilities and for a broader range of users overall.

03

In-kind technology grants

Helps organizations better understand and access nonprofit technology programs that can unlock important software, services, and digital tools.

Platform Journey

How the project is taking shape

Stage 1

Problem framing

Identifying the overlap between nonprofit accessibility needs, digital sustainability gaps, and missed technology funding opportunities.

Stage 2

Platform development

Building a public-facing platform that organizes these areas into a clearer, more practical experience for nonprofits.

Stage 3

Expansion and refinement

Continuing to grow the platform’s resources, usability, and real-world value as a long-term digital support project.

Overview

The Web-Ready Access Platform was created to give nonprofits a clearer path toward stronger digital infrastructure. Many organizations are trying to improve their websites, accessibility, and technology systems at the same time, often with limited internal capacity and limited access to specialized support.

Rather than approaching these issues in isolation, WRA brings them together into one connected platform. It is designed to help organizations better understand what strong digital practice looks like and how it can support mission delivery, usability, and long-term resilience.

This project reflects Oasis of Change’s broader mission to make digital systems more accessible, more sustainable, and more equitable. It is not simply a content page or a directory. It is a structured platform built to help nonprofits move from confusion to action.

Project Outcome

A platform that turns fragmented digital challenges into one practical support pathway.

WRA helps position accessibility, sustainability, and technology enablement as connected parts of a stronger nonprofit digital future.
